The Order Paper is a schedule showing the sequence of the matters to be dealt with in a House sitting called Orders of the Day. It is prepared by the Clerk under the direction of the Speaker by the authority of the House Business Committee. Is is the agenda through which the proceedings of the House are closely steered.
